Elevator Pitches

1

For little league or amateur baseball coaches who need to calculate and track player stats StatTracker is a baseball stats calculator that calculates and tracks stats for an entire team . Unlike keeping score on paper our product will calculate rate stats and automatically add cumulative season stats to each player.

2

For people with large physical music collections who need a way to keep track of the music they own the Record Database is a music database solution that keeps track of the physical music you own in all formats (CDs, LPs, cassettes, 8 tracks, etc.) . Unlike Collectors our product focuses solely on music and is easy to use and clutter free.

3

For baseball fans who enjoy simple text-based games the Baseball Simulator is a game that allows players to simulate a full nine inning game. Unlike most baseball simulators our product is quick to begin and easy to use.
